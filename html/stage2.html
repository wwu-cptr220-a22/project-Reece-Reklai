Real Estate Palau

Topic Overview

Are you going to buy a new home or land? This is an important question to consider as we grow up, a place to settle for yourself: possibly, a home for your future family. Real estate allows you to purchase such a transaction. This means that you can find a plot of land to build your own home or start with a home that has already built. Either way, the location of your choice determines on the luxury you get to enjoy. A home near a beach allows you to enjoy hot summer time or one near the mountains where you can expect amazing views and hikes during fall and spring: even in winter you get to snowboard! The power to buy a domain under your name allows you to enjoy its amenities while being under the protection against the four seasons and the public eye. If that is not enough, the purchase can be used as an investment to sell later down the road or allow other people to rent it. The benefits outweigh the cons in this case and thus, why real estate is such a sought out investment. If investment is not your concern, real estate will at least give you a home to settle down in.

In Palau, many people own a plot of land. In most cases, it is currently undeveloped but there are some exception where there is some sort of development that had occurred. On top of that, Palauans can own land in Palau while foreigners can only lease the land for 99 years. I want to address both of this problems! I want to make a web application that would allow Palauans to sell (optionally lease) their land amongst themselves and only lease it to foreigners. This implies two things: I want to expose Palau's land locally and internationally because Palau's real estate is currently nonexistent: I want to start the growth of Palau's real estate market.

Application's Purpose

Palau real estate web application's purpose is to allow Palauans to start selling their land locally and internationally: start the growth of Palau real estate market.

Application's Potential

  • Palauans can list their land on the website
  • Use a google map api to show the dimensions of the land while also being able to view it
  • User create an account so that they can buy, sell, and lease land
Facilitate the transaction between the buyer and seller
If it is sold to foreigners then the transaction only occurs once the foreigners signs a contract that it can only be leased once every 99 years (renewing is possible)
Keep track of the lease's current state (how much time is remaining on the contract: year remaining, month remaining, and day remaining)
